This is the famous Pizza Margherita, a true Italian classic. The story goes that it was made for Queen Margherita, and its toppings match the colours of the Italian flag: red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil. It's loved for its simple, fresh taste and is a perfect example of delicious Italian food.
