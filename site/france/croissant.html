Ingredients

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups (300ml) warm milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, cold
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour and salt. Mix until a dough forms. Knead for 5-10 minutes. Cover and chill for 1 hour.
  3. Roll dough into a rectangle. Slice cold butter and place over two-thirds of the dough. Fold the dough like a letter (in thirds).
  4. Roll out again and fold. Repeat rolling and folding 3 more times, chilling for 30 minutes between each fold.
  5. After the final chill, roll the dough into a large rectangle and cut into triangles.
  6. Roll each triangle from base to tip to form a crescent shape. Place on baking sheet, cover, and proof for 1-2 hours.
  7. Preheat oven to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C). Brush croissants with beaten egg.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown. Let cool slightly before serving.
