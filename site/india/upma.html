Upma (South Indian Rava Upma)

Serves: 2  |  Prep Time: 10 min  |  Cook Time: 15 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava / sooji)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal (optional)
  • 1 tsp chana dal (optional)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1–2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 small piece ginger, grated
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • 2½ cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Roast semolina on low flame until lightly golden and aromatic. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal and fry till golden brown.
  4. Add onions, green chilies, ginger, and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn translucent.
  5. Add 2½ cups water and salt. Bring to a boil.
  6. Slowly add roasted rava while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  7. Cover and cook for 2–3 minutes until water is absorbed and upma turns soft.
  8. Turn off the flame, add lemon juice, and fluff with a fork.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Serving:

Serve hot with coconut chutney, pickle, or a cup of tea for a comforting breakfast or evening snack.
