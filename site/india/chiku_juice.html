Chiku, also known as Sapodilla or Sapota, is a tropical fruit widely grown in India, Mexico, and the Caribbean. Traditionally eaten raw, it became popular as a juice in India for its natural sweetness and creamy texture. Over time, variations with milk, ice cream, or other fruits (like banana) have emerged, making it a beloved summer refresher.
