Kothimbir Vadi

Kothimbir Vadi is a classic Maharashtrian delicacy made from fresh coriander leaves (kothimbir) mixed with gram flour (besan) and aromatic spices. These flavorful steamed and fried bites are crispy on the outside, soft inside, and full of herby goodness. Perfect as a tea-time snack or festive treat, they represent the heart of Maharashtrian home cooking (simple, tasty and wholesome).
