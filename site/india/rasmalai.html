Ingredients

  • 1 litre full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Few saffron strands
  • 10-12 chopped pistachios & almonds
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Boil milk for malai and add lemon juice to curdle it.
  2. Strain the chenna, rinse, and hang for 30 mins.
  3. Knead the paneer and make small flat balls.
  4. Boil water with sugar and cook the balls until they double in size.
  5. Boil another litre of milk for syrup; add sugar, saffron, and cardamom.
  6. Thicken slightly and add the cooked malai balls.
  7. Cool and refrigerate before serving.
